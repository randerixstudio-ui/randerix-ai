Pakistan's First AI Architect

Randerix Studio was established in 1985 in Faisalabad, Pakistan, by Architect Rimsha — with a singular mission: to make professional architecture accessible to every Pakistani family, regardless of budget or location.

For four decades, we built homes, commercial plazas, interiors, and communities across Pakistan. In 2024, we took a historic step — launching Randerix AI, Pakistan's first artificial intelligence-powered architecture platform.

رنڈیریکس AI نے پاکستان میں آرکیٹیکچر کو ہر گھر تک پہنچانے کا خواب پورا کیا ہے۔ اب ہر پاکستانی — چاہے وہ لاہور میں ہو، فیصل آباد میں یا دبئی میں — AI کی مدد سے فوری تعمیراتی رہنمائی لے سکتا ہے۔

Today, Randerix AI serves clients across Faisalabad, Lahore, Islamabad, Karachi, and the Pakistani diaspora in UAE — providing instant AI cost estimates, floor plan generation, interior design advice, and 24/7 consultation in both Urdu and English.

We are currently in discussions with Hub71 (Abu Dhabi) to bring Randerix AI to UAE's thriving tech ecosystem, making our platform the first Pakistani PropTech to bridge the Pakistan-UAE architecture gap.

Our Journey

1985

Randerix Studio Founded

Established in Faisalabad by Architect Rimsha. First project: a 10 Marla residential home in Gulberg.

1995

Commercial Expansion

First commercial plaza project. Expanded team to 12 architects and engineers.

2010

3D Visualization Department

Launched Pakistan's first dedicated 3D architectural visualization service in Faisalabad.

2020

UAE Remote Consultations

Started serving Pakistani diaspora in UAE through remote digital consultations.

2024

Randerix AI Launched 🚀

Pakistan's first AI architecture platform launched — cost estimator, floor plan AI, interior advisor, and 24/7 AI chatbot.

2025

Hub71 Partnership Discussions

In active discussions with Hub71 Abu Dhabi for UAE expansion. Targeting 10,000 AI consultations by end of 2025.
